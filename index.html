Meal Plan for Diabetes Patient

Meal Food Carbohydrate Count
Breakfast Oatmeal with fruit and nuts 30g
Lunch Grilled chicken breast with roasted vegetables 20g
Dinner Baked salmon with quinoa and steamed broccoli 40g
Apple slices with almond butter 15g

Remember to stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water throughout the day!

Meal Planning Tips

  • Fill half your plate with non-starchy vegetables.
  • Choose lean protein sources like chicken, fish, and tofu.
  • Limit carbohydrate-containing foods to one-quarter of your plate.
  • Drink water as your primary beverage.
